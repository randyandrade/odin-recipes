Pizza!

Description

Oh, come on... EVERYBODY likes PIZZA!

Ingredients:

  • 1 1-lbs prepared frozen pizza dough ball
  • 4 ounce prepared pizza sauce
  • 1/2 cup thin sliced pepperoni
  • 2 cups shredded Mozzarella cheese

Directions:

  1. Allow frozen dough ball to thaw and rise in a covered floured bowl, about 2 hours.
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  3. Meanwhile, knead floured dough ball and stretch across a round 16" pizza screen. Very lightly dust the stretched dough with flour.
  4. Spread pizza sauce to preference across the top surface of the stretched, floured dough. Ensure you leave 1/2" border uncovered around edge of pizza.
  5. Sprinkle half the cheese over the pizza sauce, spreading evenly.
  6. Distribute sliced pepperoni evenly over the cheese and sauce on the stretched dough, then sprinkle the remainder of the Mozzarella cheese over the rest of the toppings on the dough.
  7. Bake pizza on screen in the oven for 12-15 minutes, watching for the cheese to start to brown around the edges.
  8. Allow pizza to sit at rest for at least 5 minutes to allow the toppings to properly set, and then slice and serve.
